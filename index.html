ABOUT US

Our Goal

We are a group of high schoolers who love computer science and coding! We created this project due to our collective goal to pursue CS in some capacity and to grow our knowledge of the STEM world. Through our camps, we hope to help kids learn more and expand their interests, while digesting concepts ourselves.

Mission Statement

As leadership, we will expose newcomers to new topics and guide them through it, research and practice consistently, and continue to optimize our performances as teachers through our continuous camps.
